📥 How to Submit a Position

Transparency Note: Submitting an issue does not instantly post the job. To protect our API quotas and keep the board high-signal, a maintainer must manually review your links before the automation pipeline is allowed to run.

To add new roles to the board, follow these steps:

  • Open an Issue: Navigate to our GitHub Issues tab and click "New issue."
  • Batch Your URLs: Paste the direct URLs of the job postings into the issue body. (Please use one URL per line).
  • Wait for Review: A maintainer will manually review your links. If they fit the board's strict scope, the maintainer will apply the job-submission label.
  • Automated Parsing: That label triggers our background "Data Clerk" to scrape the listings and generate the necessary data.
  • Live Deployment: Once we merge the generated data, the roles will appear live on the board!
🛡️ About Threaty / Purpose [ Hide ]

Threaty is a curated job board built to filter out general engineering noise. This user-submitted board isolates high-signal positions focused on Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI), Detection Engineering, Incident Response (IR), and of course, Thrunting. By surfacing region constraints directly in the dashboard, Threaty helps you identify roles that match your specific geographic and authorization requirements instantly.

Created by Lacey Kasten.

Powered by: GitHub Actions, Gemini AI (Data Clerk), & Vanilla JavaScript.

Threaty is an open-source project. Follow the project or contribute on GitHub.
